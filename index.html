About

Hi, I'm Evan. I am an Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) master's student at UC Berkeley, advised by Professor Jiantao Jiao. I also completed my bachelor's degree in computer science at Berkeley.


My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) for fine-tuning LLMs. Currently, much of my efforts revolve around reward model training and benchmarking.


I am also a Research Engineer at Nexusflow, where I work on training LLMs like Athene-70B. I also work with LMSYS, mainly on analyzing Chatbot Arena and building LLM benchmarks.
