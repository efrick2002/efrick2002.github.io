About

Hi, I'm Evan. I am a member of technical staff at LMArena. I finished my master's in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at UC Berkeley in May 2025, advised by Professor Jiantao Jiao. I also completed my bachelor's degree in computer science at Berkeley. In addition, I've been lucky to mentored by Ion Stoica, Banghua Zhu, Anastasios Angelopoulos, and Wei-Lin Chiang.


Previously, my research has focused on Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) for fine-tuning LLMs–– much my efforts revolved around reward model training and benchmarking.


During my master's I was also a Research Engineer at Nexusflow, where I trained open-weights LLMs like Athene-70B and Athene-V2-Chat. These days at LMArena, I'm focused on modeling human preference and shaping data quality.
